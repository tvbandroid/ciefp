# Hey there 👋
<img align="right" width="360" src="https://github.com/ciefp/ciefp/blob/main/assets/ciefp-logo.png?raw=true" alt="ciefp" />

I'm **ciefp** — long-time satellite enthusiast and Enigma2 developer.  
I've been building channel lists since the Enigma1 era and for more than a decade on Enigma2.  
My settings/lists are officially shipped with almost every major image: **OpenATV, OpenPLi, OpenViX, PurE2, VTi, OpenBH, OpenSPA, Egami** and many others — used daily by **over 100,000 users** worldwide.

In the last couple of years (and especially in the past year with the help of AI), I’ve focused on writing **free, lightweight and highly practical** Enigma2 plugins and tools for IPTV bouquets, EPG handling, OSCam configuration and advanced satellite analysis (T2-MI, Abertis, multistream, etc.).

Everything I release is and always will be **100% free and open-source**.
